About the NAFCoin Project

The NAFCoin project aims to set up a modular refinery which can be expanded easily in the future. The products of interest to the NAFCoin project are Diesel and Aviation Kerosene. The NAFCoin token is an ERC20-based security token with an initial price of 0.10 EUR. The dividend that would be paid against the token holdings would be 0.23 EUR to 0.68 EUR over 5 years. Such dividends would be governed by Smart Contracts and paid in Ether. The token would be tradeable on exchanges.

Ghana and west african markets

Ghana

  • Ghana is a politically stable country after its transition to multi-party democracy in 1992.

  • 25,5 million people live at Ghana, the majority being English speaking.

  • The Government of Ghana has outlined a bold vision to make Ghana a hub for refined petroleum products for West Africa. The hub will be built in three phases over a 13-year period starting from 2018 and to be completed by 2030.

West African Markets

  • The West-African subregion requires about 850,000 barrels of refined fuels per day to meet its needs.

  • Currently, the total refinery capacity (for refineries) in the region is estimated at 500,000 barrels per day, leaving a shortfall gap of 350,000 barrels per day.

  • It is cost efficient to refine crude produced in the region in refineries located in the region since regional market demand is sizeable to justify setting up large scale refinery capacities.
